About Me

My name is Meghan Themistocle, I am a 22-year-old, soon to be, graduate, with no real passion for something. I will be officially adulting in the next few months, and with all the stress, excitement, and overwhelming change that is about to come, I feel that I will need that one or few things to reminded me of myself and to help me stay grounded when the chaos ensues.

I call myself a "jack of trades but master of none" because I do enjoy doing a lot of things, like drawing, ceramics, playing soccer, basketball, cooking, and so much more. The problem is that I've never really taken the time to explore those things and establish what I'm really passionate about.

What to Expect

With this blog I hope to try new activities and experiences to find my passion and start my passion project. Throughout my journey there will be a lot of successes and A LOT of failures, but I hope to document and share my stories with you, and maybe help you find your own passion project.
